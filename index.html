About Us

Monarda Treats is a local business in Ottawa made for all baked goods lovers. Our fine selection of cakes, breads, croissants, and pies have won serveral awards over the years.

Our Mission

We have always been a community based business, and we have no intention of ever changing that. We love bringing people together and hosting events to share our love of the city (and yummy treats) together as one big family!

Activities

This location offers a beautiful venue to host anything from local music nights to full wedding ceremonies thanks to our backyard garden. Capacity up to 90 people!
